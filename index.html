Business Blog Post Ideas

Business blogging is big business (and it’s our business). There are lots of lists of general “business blog post topics” floating around online but generic topic ideas that can apply to any business in any industry aren’t really what most brands like to fill their blogs with. There are many benefits of business blogging, but one of the most powerful is the ability to position yourself as an expert in your field by providing insightful, current, and most importantly accurate industry relevant content. You’re not going to be able to do that with something you’d find on a generic “business blog topics” list (although we have one of those too). Lifestyle Blog Post Ideas

The term lifestyle blog is one that is often mistakenly used interchangeably with “personal blog” – but they’re not the same thing. In a nutshell: The topics on a personal blog focus on the blogger as a person. Their experiences, and their thoughts are the overall subject of the blog. Think Carry Bradshaw from Sex And The City. A lifestyle blogger writes posts with topics that focus on the reader. They contain news stories, recommendations, and other content that focuses on the specific kind of “lifestyle” the blog is about. For example, here’s a list of our various different lifestyle blog post ideas:

Casino & Gambling
Love, Relationships, & Dating
Parenting & Family
Yoga
Travel
Christian
